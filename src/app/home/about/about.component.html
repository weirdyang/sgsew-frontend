Our Origins


Welcome to Collab, your number one source for all things IT. We're dedicated to providing you the very best of our IT hardware and services, with an emphasis on being timely, accessible and up-to-date.

Founded in 2015 by Weiyang and Rex, Collab has come a long way from its beginnings in Bedok Princess Center. When they first started out, their passion for IT hardware and services drove them to start their own business.

Currently, we have spread our wings from our physical stores to a more modern, virtual approach. We sell our products through renown platforms in Singapore such as Carousell, Shopee and Lazada.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you.

Behind the Logo

Our logo symbolises the goals and aspirations of what our company wishes to reach. We aim to stay connected with everyone, from local IT businesses to global corporations, in order to provide the best possible products and services out there.

Everyone is welcome to join us in our quest and every type of IT product, from simple gadgets to the most complicated of systems, are welcome on our shelves.

Flexibility is key to survive in this ever-changing global environment, even the big-hitters of yesterday can fall if they don't adapt. Even if we do try to adapt, we must strive to be nimble about it or it will not matter much.

We will strive to engage with each and everyone to achieve those goals, and then we can breakthrough as leaders of this industry!
